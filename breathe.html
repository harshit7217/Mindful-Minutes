🧘‍♂️ Breathing Techniques

Choose a technique below to begin your mindful moment

☁️ Box Breathing

Inhale – Hold – Exhale – Hold (4 seconds each). Great for focus and calm.

🌙 4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale for 4, hold for 7, exhale for 8. Promotes deep relaxation and sleep.

🌊 Equal Breathing

Inhale and exhale for the same duration. Balances and grounds the mind.

🔥 Extended Exhale

Exhale longer than you inhale to engage your body’s relaxation response.
