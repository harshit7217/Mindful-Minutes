🌿 About Mindful Minutes

Mindful Minutes is a wellness web app designed to help you breathe better, reflect deeper, and live more intentionally.

👨‍💻 Built by

Hi, I'm Harshit Chaudhary, Paridhi Singh, Prasang Singh, Simran Joshi — a developer passionate about blending mindfulness with technology. This app is my way of sharing calm, clarity, and creativity with the world.

✨ Features

  • Breathing techniques: Box, 4-7-8, Equal, Extended Exhale
  • Mood tracking with visual graphs
  • Gratitude journaling
  • Personalized daily intentions and goals
